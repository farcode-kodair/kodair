Tax Calculator

This calculator estimates your business taxes based on your income and expenses. The estimated tax is calculated using a flat tax rate of 20% on the taxable income (income minus expenses).

Shipping Calculator

Estimate your shipping costs based on package weight and distance. The cost is calculated with a rate of $5 per kilogram of weight and $0.10 per kilometer of distance.

Loan Payment Calculator

Calculate your monthly loan payments using this tool. Enter the loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan term to estimate your monthly payment. The calculation uses the following formula:

Monthly Payment = (P * r) / (1 - (1 + r)^(-n))

where:

  • P: Principal loan amount
  • r: Monthly interest rate (annual rate divided by 12)
  • n: Number of monthly payments (loan term in years multiplied by 12)
